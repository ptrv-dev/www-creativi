About us

Let's get to know each other

In 2004, M7Alpha Creative Studios was a one-man army spearheaded by Maurice Aquilina; today, it’s a melting pot of local talents committed to bringing out the best in each other and seasoning the work we deliver with a sprinkle of surprise.

We’re a circle of professionals from diverse backgrounds united behind a philosophy that puts performance before power. M7Alpha Creative Studios includes both apprentices and masters of their craft, but we adhere to a flat hierarchy that eliminates excess levels and layers.

A flat structure elevates each employee’s responsibility inside the organisation and eliminates excess management layers to improve coordination and communication. Fewer levels between employees improve the decision-making process among staff, frees up our budget, and eliminates bureaucracy.
